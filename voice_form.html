Your voice

What if people who most urgently need support are also the ones least likely to speak up?

If you've been close to serious illness, as a patient, a loved one, or someone who cares, this is for you.

Sharing your experience could help someone else.

There are no wrong answers. Even one sentence helps.

You may respond anonymously, or leave your email address if you are open to connecting.

*Only the founder will see this. She may send a personal thank-you or reach out to connect.
